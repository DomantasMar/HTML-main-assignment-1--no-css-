• VALORANT

    Valorant is a free-to-play competitive 5 versus 5 character-based tactical shooter that has been released on June 2nd, 2020, developed by Riot games. It's setting is a near-future Earth, where you team up with 4 other players to fight against 5 other players in round-based combat with an agent of your choosing
